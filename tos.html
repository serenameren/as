Service Policy

Eligibility

Anyone can access and use our exchange service without restrictions. We do not impose discriminatory criteria or rejection policies.

Rates and Fees

Our exchange operates on a dynamic rate system, continuously updating based on cryptocurrency market fluctuations. Once a payment is received, the rate is locked for 45 minutes. If no payment is detected within this period, the rate will update every 45 minutes until payment is made.

Customer Payments

There are no minimum or maximum amount restrictions for sending payments. However, if a customer sends an amount exceeding our conversion balance limits, they will be automatically refunded. Minimum and maximum balance limits are displayed before creating an order.

Our Payments

We process payments to customers once their transaction has received sufficient blockchain confirmations and is within our operational balance limits. These limits are disclosed to users before initiating a transaction.

Refunds

All refund operations are fully automated and incur no additional fees.

Support

Users can contact support via the Telegram link on our website. Each order also includes a dedicated support chat, allowing users to receive transaction-specific assistance. Response times depend on current workload but will not exceed 12 hours.

Prohibited Jurisdictions

None. Our system does not log IP addresses or track customer locations, ensuring full anonymity.

Privacy Policy

We do not collect or store customer metadata. IP logging is disabled across all service endpoints, including reverse proxies, caching servers, and backend systems. Exchange-related transaction data is deleted immediately after completion, making it irretrievable.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.
